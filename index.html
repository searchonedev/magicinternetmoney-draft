About MAGIC INTERNET MONEY

The Bitcoin Wizard meme was created by Mavensbot in 2013 as an advertisement on Reddit. In 2023, Ordinals were created and the BRC20 token $WZRD was born. We have now migrated from BRC20 to the Runes protocol to become MAGIC INTERNET MONEY token.

The Lore

February 2013

A contest was ran on Reddit fro a new ad to try ad attract users to /r/bitcoin subreddit. The bitcoin Wizard design won the contest and the ad started running. Subscribers went from 50-75k in weeks. The ad was so good that it ranked top on /r/ad subreddit and was the best performing ad on reddit for a long time. Many OG Bitcoiners from the 2013 era discovered Bitcoin through this ad. @udiWertheimer (thought leader in the Ordinals space included.

Lore Subsection 2

There is also evidence that the Bitcoin Wizard caused the 2nd major Bitcoin bullrun. 22 days after the ad was published, Bitcoin went up from $287 to $1132 and reached a new ATH, it was the best-performing Reddit ad at the time.

Lore Subsection 3

The Bitcoin Wizard became the first Bitcoin meme, shared by many of the most influential figures in Crypto culture. Here's a video that compiles this. Included are CZ of Binance, the Dogecoin founder BillyM2K, and there even was a South Park episode highlighting the ad.

Lore Subsection 4

The Bitcoin Wizard Ordinal collection was founded by the same artist who created the original ad and the first meme on Bitcoin. The collection has a small supply of 1337 Wizards launching on the anniversaryof the original ad going live (Feb 2013).

February 2023

The $WZRD token is part of the Bitcoin Wizards project, which uses the BRC-20 token standard on the Bitcoin blockchain. This standard simplifies the creation and transfer of tokens, making it easier for projects like Bitcoin Wizards to operate on the Bitcoin network.
